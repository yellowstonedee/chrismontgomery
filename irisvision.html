IRISVISION

UI Design, Branding

IrisVision is a low vision aid designed and developed specifically for patients with macular degeneration and other low vision disorders. It utilizes the latest VR technology to help normalize vision in patients. Other low vision solutions are often very expensive, cumbersome, or hard to use. IrisVision aims to fill the gap with an affordable, powerful, and easy to use consumer device.

Learn more about IrisVision at theirisvision.com

THE WORK

Designing the UI for users with low vision posed a unique and difficult challenge. Users with macular degeneration are still able to see, however their ability to see in sharp detail is greatly diminished, particularly in the center. Users have the ability to customize a wide variety options, such as bubble size, bubble shape, magnification level, brightness, etc. Due to the highly customizable nature of IrisVision, low vision patients need to be able to navigate the menus themselves. In creating the menus, I used a combination of high contrast, size increase with color highlighting, and positioning to easily allow users to know what menu item was selected.
