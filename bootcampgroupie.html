BOOTCAMP GROUPIE

Information Architecture, Navigation Update, UI Re-Design

Bootcamp Groupie is a fitness app where users join short workout programs called bootcamps. During these bootcamps, users are given a daily workout and meal plan to follow. Weekly check ins allow users to track their progress by updating their weight, measurements, and body pictures. Unlike many other workout program apps, users join bootcamps with others to help encourage each other to keep going and meet their goals.

THE WORK

Bootcamp Groupie launched and was having some fundamental issues when it came to user experience and navigation, as well as an outdated and lackluster design. I was brought on to re-imagine and re-design the layout as well as the look and feel. My first step was to re-design the overall navigation. Users were having a hard time finding out when they needed to take an action. Users are required to take many actions at different times during the day, so having these call to actions hidden behind a menu was detrimental to user retention. I decided to use a tab bar navigation because it is easier for users to get where they need to go. This also allowed each section to have its own call to action that was easily visible at all times.
