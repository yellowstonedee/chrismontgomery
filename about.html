ABOUT ME

My name is Chris Montgomery. I'm a UX/UI Designer for mobile and web applications. Originally from Vermont, I graduated from Rochester Institute of Technology with a BFA in Visual Media. I initially started my college career pursuing photography but quickly developed a love for design. From there I discovered UX/UI Design and knew I found my calling. I specialize in low fidelity wireframes, user research, sitemaps, and visual design/style guide creation.

In my free time I enjoy playing video games, watching movies (especially horror), and juggling.

My favorite dogs are pugs and bulldogs, but all dogs are great!

I enjoy spaghetti, burritos, and Thai food.
